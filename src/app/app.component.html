What do we do?

Marine Solutions is a specialized construction and engineering firm focused on building and maintaining waterfront, hydraulic, navigation, and bridge structures. We are a certified small, woman-owned, disadvantaged business enterprise (WBE/DBE) and are fully insured for general, marine, and professional liability. Our headquarters are in Kentucky with offices in Maryland, New Jersey, New York, and Ohio. We offer our services throughout the United States and our valued clients include federal, state and municipal agencies, architectural and engineering firms, construction companies, and industrial entities.
